About Sunset Takeaway

Sunset Takeaway is a small, family–run business that has been serving the local community since 2020. Our goal is simple: to make fast food that still tastes homemade.

We prepare our pizza dough in-house every day, use fresh toppings and cook to order so that your food is hot and crisp when you arrive to collect it.

What Makes Us Different?

  • ✔ Freshly made pizza dough and burger patties.
  • ✔ Vegetarian and halal options available.
  • ✔ Simple phone ordering to keep things quick.
  • ✔ Friendly staff who know our regular customers by name.

This site is the first design stage of our online presence and provides information for customers and stakeholders before we move on to adding online ordering in the future.
